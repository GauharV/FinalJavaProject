# Samuel Taiwo, Gauhar Veeravalli
## ZING ZING ZINGBAH RACING
### What we are building:
**We are making a racing game similar to that of mario kart, but Boohbah themed. The game will have a racing engine and a character selection consisting of around 5 characters.**  
### Why is this a Java 2 project?
**Concepts like lists, stacks, and queues as well as generics will be implemented into this project. Concepts from Java I will be implemented as well**  
### What is your MVP?
**Our MVP is without a doubt our racing engine. Without it, there is no game. Other things like character selection are crucial but not our top priority.** 
### Stretch Goals (optional)
**With extra time we would add animations and possibly make the game feel more alive**  
### AI Use Plan
**We do plan to use AI.**
